My sound is heavily influenced by psytrance, trance and industrial techno. I aim to bring a unique and psychedelic twist to the techno scene. I am constantly pushing my own boundaries and evolving my sound to be heavier and more experimental. My main focus is to create an immersive experience for the listener. I find my home in the underground techno scene, and I believe my heavy and psychedelic sound sets me apart.
